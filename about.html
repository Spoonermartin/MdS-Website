Who We Are

A growth studio built on business sense, not just design taste.

MdS Websites is a founder-led digital growth studio in Cambridge. We combine 12+ years of design and development with 30+ years of real-world business and management experience.

Our Story

It started with one question: why don't most websites actually work?

MdS Websites began the way many of the best studios do, building sites for our own projects, then for family, then for friends, then for their friends. What became clear early on was that most small-business websites fail not because they look bad, but because nobody asked the strategic questions first: Who is this for? How do they search? Why would they choose you?

So we built our entire practice around answering those questions before we design anything. Today we work with growing organisations across the East of England, from Cambridge colleges to coaching practices, coach operators to legal advisers, helping them build stronger brands, attract more customers and grow their revenue.

Deliberately small, deliberately senior: when you work with MdS, you work directly with the strategist who designs and builds your project. No hand-offs, no juniors learning on your budget.

Mission & Values

What we believe

Research before pixels

Strategy isn't a luxury add-on. Every project starts with understanding your market, your competitors and your customers.

Results over awards

A website's job is enquiries, bookings and revenue. We measure our work by what it earns you, not how it photographs.

Plain English, always

No jargon, no mystification. You'll understand every recommendation we make and every invoice we send.

Honest scoping

If a refresh will do the job, we won't sell you a rebuild. Long-term trust beats short-term revenue, every time.

Craft matters

The details, typography, speed and accessibility, are where premium feel and real performance come from.

Growth is a partnership

Launch is the start, not the finish. The best results come from continuous optimisation, together.
