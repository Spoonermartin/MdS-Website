Refresh or Rebrand?

Already have a brand that's holding you back?

Not every business needs to start from scratch. Sometimes a focused refresh — sharpening the logo, modernising the palette, tightening the messaging, delivers most of the impact at a fraction of the cost.

  • Brand refresh: evolve what you have. Keep the equity, lose the dated feel.
  • Rebrand: reposition entirely, ideal when your business has outgrown its original identity or changed direction.

We'll review your existing brand honestly and recommend the route that makes commercial sense, not the one with the bigger invoice.

Questions

Branding FAQs

Do I really need branding, or just a website?

They work together. Your website gets you found; your brand gets you chosen. If your visual identity is inconsistent or dated, even a brilliant website will under-perform, because visitors make trust judgements in seconds. Many clients combine a brand refresh with a new website for exactly this reason.

How long does a branding project take?

A focused identity project typically takes 3–6 weeks; a full rebrand with strategy, guidelines and rollout support takes longer. We'll give you a clear timeline after the discovery call.

What do I receive at the end?

All logo files in every format you'll need, your colour and typography specifications, and a practical brand guidelines document, everything required for any designer, printer or developer to apply your brand correctly.

Can you rebrand us without losing existing customers?

Yes, that's what brand strategy is for. We identify the equity worth keeping (name recognition, colours customers associate with you) and evolve around it, with a rollout plan that brings your audience along rather than confusing them.
