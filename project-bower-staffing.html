Professional Services · Recruitment

A dual-audience recruitment website that serves employers and candidates equally.

Bower Staffing Group needed a single website to serve two very different audiences, guiding each one to the right conversion path within seconds.

Professional ServicesRecruitment

The Challenge

One business, two audiences, one website, and each audience needed a different experience.

Bower Staffing Group works with both employers looking to fill roles and candidates looking for their next position. Most recruitment websites fail one audience or the other. The challenge was to design a site that immediately routes each visitor toward the right content and conversion path, without confusion or compromise.

Our Solution

Dual-journey UX with a dynamic job board built for long-term self-management.

We designed a clean, professional website with a dual-journey architecture that separates the employer and candidate experience from the first interaction. A dynamic job board allows the client to add, update and remove vacancies without developer support, giving full internal control over their recruitment content.

  • Website design and development
  • Dual-journey UX architecture for employers and candidates
  • Dynamic job posting board with full client control
  • Service and sector pages for employer audiences
  • Candidate-facing role search and application pathways
  • Mobile-responsive, fast-loading build

The Outcome

Clearer journeys for employers and candidates, with full in-house control.

With a structured dual-audience website and an easy-to-manage job board, Bower Staffing Group can now advertise roles quickly, guide both audiences to the right place, and update their content independently, without relying on developer support.
