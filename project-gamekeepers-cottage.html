A charming direct-booking site that eliminates platform commissions.

Hospitality & TourismHoliday RentalDirect Booking

The Challenge

A genuinely special cottage was giving away margin to platforms it didn't need.

Gamekeeper's Cottage is a cherished rural holiday property with a loyal guest following and a distinctive character that sets it apart from generic rentals. Despite this, the owners were dependent on listing platforms that charged commission on every booking and controlled the guest relationship. They needed a website that could capture bookings directly, without losing the warmth and personality that makes the cottage special.

Our Solution

An atmospheric direct-booking site that sells the experience first.

We designed a website that leads with the story of the cottage, its history, its setting, its character, rather than just the facilities. The booking journey is simple and direct, with a clear calendar and pricing that make it easy to check availability and confirm a stay without leaving the site.

  • Website design and development
  • Atmospheric copywriting and content structure
  • Direct booking calendar and availability integration
  • Gallery presentation that showcases the property
  • Mobile-responsive build optimised for booking on any device
  • SEO content for holiday cottage searches in the area

The Outcome

Growing share of commission-free direct bookings.

By owning the booking relationship, the owners of Gamekeeper's Cottage now retain more revenue per booking, build a direct guest relationship, and present their property in a way that no listing platform could replicate.
