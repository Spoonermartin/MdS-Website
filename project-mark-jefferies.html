Events & Aviation · Digital Presence

A full theme rebuild for an established air display brand.

Mark Jefferies is a world-class air display pilot with an established brand, but a legacy website that wasn't mobile-responsive and wasn't serving its audience well.

Events & AviationSpecialist

The Challenge

A world-class pilot with a website that didn't reflect the calibre of his work.

Mark Jefferies Airdisplays is a respected name in air display performance, but the existing website was built on a non-responsive legacy theme that performed poorly on mobile, presented news content ineffectively, and did little to communicate the excitement and professionalism of the display brand.

Our Solution

A full rebuild, responsive, modern and built to showcase the spectacle.

We rebuilt the website theme from the ground up, delivering a fully mobile-responsive site with an improved homepage that showcases news, upcoming displays and media content prominently. The rebuild preserves the brand's existing identity while dramatically improving the user experience across all devices.

  • Full WordPress theme rebuild
  • Fully mobile-responsive layout across all pages
  • Improved homepage with news and media showcase
  • Better display schedule and event presentation
  • Optimised media gallery for video and photography
  • Improved page speed and performance

The Outcome

Fully mobile-responsive. Better engagement. Brand elevated.

The rebuilt website gives Mark Jefferies Airdisplays a platform that matches the quality of the display brand, mobile-responsive, fast, and structured to showcase news, media and upcoming events in a way that engages fans and event organisers alike.
